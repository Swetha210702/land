About Srijan

Not waiting
for permission.

Srijan Tiwari is a speaker, educator, and entrepreneur who believes the industrial world deserves better talent infrastructure. At an age when most people are still finding their footing, Srijan is already keynoting at national conferences, teaching AI at IIT Madras, and co-founding a startup that's reshaping how the industry hires and certifies.

His talks blend raw energy with deep industry insight — whether he's breaking down diffusion models to a classroom or challenging a room of NDT professionals to rethink certification standards.
